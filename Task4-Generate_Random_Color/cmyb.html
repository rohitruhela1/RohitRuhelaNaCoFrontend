Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Black (CMYK)

CMYK is a subtractive color model used in color printing. It works by absorbing light rather than emitting it, unlike the RGB model.

The primary colors Cyan, Magenta, and Yellow mix to create different colors. Adding Black (Key) enhances depth and contrast.
